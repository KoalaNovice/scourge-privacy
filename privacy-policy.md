# Privacy Policy – Scourge AI
**Effective date:** April 19, 2026  
**Last updated:** April 19, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how **Scourge AI** (“Scourge”, “we”, “us”) collects, uses, shares, and protects information when you use our application and related services (the “Service”).

> This template is provided for operational convenience and should be reviewed by counsel for your specific legal/organizational requirements.

---

1) Who This Service Is For
Scourge is an **operator-facing investigative and supervision tool** intended for **authorized personnel only** (for example, trained operators working under internal policies and applicable law). It is not intended for public consumer use.

---

2) What We Collect
Depending on configuration and use, we may collect:

 A. Operator Account Data
- Name, email address, password hash/salt (not your raw password)
- Authentication/session tokens
- Operator preferences (for example, timezone, notification settings)
- Uploaded verification documents (if your deployment enables this feature)

 B. Connected Account Data (Linked Accounts)
When you link a social/messaging account (for example, Instagram, Telegram), we may store:
- Platform identifiers (page IDs, account IDs, user IDs)
- Account display name/username
- Configuration and persona/context settings
- Access credentials/tokens required to operate the integration (stored securely where applicable)

 C. Conversation and Evidence Data
- Message content (inbound and outbound)
- Timestamps, message IDs, conversation/thread IDs
- Attachments and media (images/videos/files) and associated metadata
- AI-generated summaries, risk/urgency scoring outputs, and decision context
- Audit logs and evidence/chain-of-custody records (for example, file views/exports and reasons)

 D. Device/Usage Data
- IP address (for security/audit logging)
- Browser/user-agent data
- Operational logs for reliability and troubleshooting

---

3) Where Data Comes From
- Operators who enter information into the Service
- Messaging platforms you connect (via their APIs/webhooks)
- Automated processing performed by the Service (including AI-generated outputs)

---

4) How We Use Information
We use information to:
- Authenticate operators and control access
- Operate connected messaging accounts and process inbound/outbound communications
- Provide supervision workflows (queues, triage, escalation, approvals)
- Generate and store evidence/audit artifacts (including exports and integrity hashes)
- Improve reliability, security monitoring, and debugging
- Comply with legal obligations and internal policies

---

5) Legal Basis (If Applicable)
If your deployment is subject to laws requiring a legal basis (for example, certain privacy regimes), processing may rely on one or more of:
- Compliance with legal obligations
- Legitimate interests in safety, security, and investigation
- Performance of tasks carried out in the public interest (where applicable)
- Consent where required (implementation dependent)

---

6) How We Share Information
We may share information:
**Within your organization**: with authorized operators and administrators with a need-to-know
**With service providers**: hosting, email delivery, security tooling, and AI processing providers acting as processors
**With legal authorities**: when required by law, legal process, or as part of authorized investigative workflows
**During emergencies**: where necessary to prevent harm

We do not sell personal information.

---

7) AI Processing
The Service may use third-party AI services to generate:
- draft responses
- summaries
- risk/urgency assessments
- media descriptions (where enabled)

AI outputs are used to assist operator workflows and may be stored in the Service along with related audit information.

---

8) Data Retention
Retention depends on your organization’s operational and legal requirements. In general:
- Conversation and evidence records may be retained for **auditability, investigation, and compliance**.
- Audit logs and chain-of-custody records may be retained longer than operational logs.
- Tokens/credentials are retained while an account remains linked and may be revoked/removed when unlinked (implementation dependent).

---

9) Security
We use administrative, technical, and physical safeguards designed to protect information, including:
- access controls and authentication
- audit logging
- secure storage practices for credentials (where applicable)
- least-privilege operational access

No system can be guaranteed 100% secure.

---

10) Children’s Privacy
The Service is intended for authorized operators and is **not directed to children**.

---

11) Your Rights and Choices
Depending on jurisdiction and your organization’s policies, you may have rights to:
- access, correct, or delete certain personal information
- restrict or object to certain processing

Requests can be submitted using the contact method below. We may require verification before acting on requests.

---

12) Data Deletion Instructions (Meta Platform Requirement)
If you want information associated with your platform account removed from the Service, contact us with:
- the platform (e.g., Instagram)
- your platform user ID/username
- the linked account name (if known)
- the reason for the request

We will respond consistent with applicable law and organizational policies.

---

13) International Transfers
If your deployment stores or processes data outside your jurisdiction, information may be transferred across borders subject to applicable safeguards.
